Precautions to Be Taken

  • Avoid unnecessary exposure to radiation from medical imaging or environmental sources. If working in radiation-prone environments (such as hospitals or labs),
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in antioxidants, fruits, vegetables, and whole grains to support brain health.
  • Physical activity strengthens the immune system, making it more effective in detecting and eliminating abnormal cells
  • Reducing exposure to environmental toxins, tobacco, and excessive alcohol consumption is crucial for maintaining overall health and minimizing the risk of various diseases, including brain tumors..
  • Stress and depression can have profound effects on overall health, influencing both mental and physical well-being. Chronic stress leads to the release of hormones like cortisol, which, when elevated for prolonged periods, can weaken the immune system,
  • Regular health monitoring and timely medical consultations play a crucial role in early disease detection and preventive care. Many health conditions, including chronic illnesses and certain cancers, develop silently without noticeable symptoms in their early stages .
